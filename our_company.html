Company Profile

Our Company

Gulf Concreting Products was established in 2003 and Crown Concreting Products in 2005 to carry out the marketing and sales of concrete durability additives and waterproof membranes for the construction industry in the Gulf region.

Gulf and Crown Concreting Products represent a number of international companies for various Gulf countries. Among the represented companies are Surtreat International USA, Mix UK England, English Indian Clays Limited India, Whitaker Chemicals England, and Stirling Lloyd England. The products include migrating corrosion inhibitors - Surtreat TPS; Integral corrosion inhibitor and Calcium Nitrite SCI-S; Polypropylene fibres - Fiberon for concrete crack Control; Metacon Metakaolin, Microsilica, Fly-Ash; Permare polysulphide epoxy membranes for portable water structures and Eliminator/Integritank; methyl methacrylate (MMA) membranes for waterproofing bridge decks, basements, sewage tanks and oil storage tanks.

Gulf and Crown Concreting Products client list includes all the major readymix concrete companies and many of the major contractors in the Gulf region and in particular the UAE. Among the clients for the products are Dubai Municipality, Abu Dhabi Municipality, Abu Dhabi Water and Electricity Department, ADNOC, ADCO, Qatar Petroleum, Oman Ports Authority, The Department of Social Services-Abu Dhabi, Dubai Electricity and Water Authority, Nakheel and many other private and government authorities in the region with the numbers increasing all the time.

Gulf and Crown Concreting Products have offices in Fujairah, Abu Dhabi and Dubai. The companies also have warehousing in Abu Dhabi and Dubai and have a dedicated locally based sales and marketing staff.

Gulf and Crown Concreting Products have frequent visits to the region on a regular basis from the principle companies Technical Engineers.

As well as sales and marketing Gulf and Crown Concreting Products also assist their clients and their customers with technical support and advice.
